What is COP26?

COP 26 is the UNâ€™s 26th Climate Change Conferences. These conferences are meetings of many countries to discuss the progress of meeting the goals established in the Paris Agreement. They discuss future policies to ensure that we will meet these goals by the necessary time. In addition to the goals discussed in the Paris Agreement, the UN also has 17 Sustainable Development Goals. As discussed in COP26, the world will need to reduce our greenhouse gas emissions by fifty percent within eight years. Carbon dioxide is one of the most common greenhouse gasses, so it is important for these countries to lower their carbon footprint.
